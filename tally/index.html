Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tally?
Tally is a simple multi-counter app. Create counters for anything — reps at the gym, drinks of water, inventory counts, game scores. Tap to increment, swipe to reset. Available on iPhone and Apple Watch.
Does Tally work on Apple Watch?
Yes. Tally includes a Watch complication so you can see and update your counters directly from your wrist without pulling out your phone.
Is Tally free?
Tally will offer a free tier with basic counters. Pro features like unlimited counters, custom icons, and Watch complications will be available via a one-time purchase.
When is Tally launching?
Tally is currently in development. Follow us for updates — we'll announce the launch date soon.
How is my data stored?
All counter data is stored locally on your device. No account required, no cloud sync (yet). Keep iCloud device backup enabled to protect your data.
