Frequently asked questions

What is Spark Pro?
Spark Pro unlocks unlimited habits, detailed stats & insights, home screen widgets, Apple Watch complications, Siri shortcuts, and custom app icons. Free users can track up to 3 habits forever.
How do forgiving streaks work?
Missing a day dips your streak health — it never resets to zero. Come back the next day and it starts recovering. We believe momentum matters more than perfection.
How do I restore my Pro subscription?
Open Spark → tap the gear icon → Restore Purchases. Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID and works across all your devices.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Open Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Spark. You can cancel anytime. Your Pro access continues until the end of the current billing period.
Is my data backed up?
Habit data is stored locally on your device. We recommend keeping iCloud device backup enabled in your iOS settings to protect your data.
The app is crashing. What should I do?
Try force-quitting and reopening the app. If the issue persists, check for an update in the App Store. Still having trouble? Contact us below with your device model and iOS version.
