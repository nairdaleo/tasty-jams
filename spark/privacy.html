Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Overview

Spark: ADHD Habit Tracker ("the App") is developed by Tasty Jam ("we", "us"), a sole proprietorship based in British Columbia, Canada.

Data we collect

All habit data — habits, completions, streaks, and reminders — is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal habit data on any server.

The only data we collect is through Apple's standard infrastructure:

  • Purchase and subscription data — handled entirely by Apple. We receive anonymised purchase confirmation but never your payment details.
  • Crash reports — if you opt in to share diagnostics with Apple, anonymised crash data may be shared with us via App Store Connect.
  • Support contact — if you contact us via the support form, we collect the name, email, and message you provide solely to respond to your inquiry.

What we don't collect

  • No remote tracking of habits, completions, or streaks
  • No advertising SDKs or analytics trackers
  • No sale of data to third parties
  • No access to contacts, camera, photos, or location
  • No user profiling or advertising data

Notifications

Reminders are scheduled as local notifications on your device. No data is sent to any server — they are handled entirely by iOS.

Subscriptions

Spark Pro subscriptions are processed by Apple. We never see or store your payment information. Management, cancellation, and refunds are handled through your Apple ID settings.

Apple Watch & widgets

Habit data shared with the Apple Watch app and home screen widget is transferred via iOS App Groups — a secure local communication mechanism on your device. No internet connection is used.

Children's privacy

Spark does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since no personal data is collected from any user, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at support@tastyjam.ca.
