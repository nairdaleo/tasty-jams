Tasty Jams/ Spark/ Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Spark: ADHD Habit Tracker ("Spark", "we", "us") is developed and operated by Tasty Jams (Adrian Leon), a sole proprietorship based in British Columbia, Canada. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data Spark collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data we collect

Spark is designed with privacy first. All habit data — including your habits, completions, streaks, and reminders — is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal habit data on any server.

The only data we collect is through Apple's standard App Store infrastructure:

  • Purchase and subscription data — handled entirely by Apple. We receive anonymised purchase confirmation but never your payment details.
  • Crash reports — if you opt in to share diagnostics with Apple, anonymised crash data may be shared with us via App Store Connect. This contains no personally identifiable information.
  • Support contact — if you contact us via the support form, we collect the name, email, and message you provide. This is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not track your habits, completions, or streaks remotely
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or analytics trackers
  • We do not sell your data to any third party
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, photos, or location
  • We do not build profiles or use data for advertising

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Spark schedules local notifications on your device. No data is sent to any server to deliver these notifications — they are handled entirely by iOS.

Subscriptions

Spark Pro subscriptions are processed by Apple. We never see or store your payment information. Subscription management, cancellation, and refunds are handled through your Apple ID settings.

Apple Watch & Widgets

Habit data shared with the Apple Watch app and home screen widget is transferred via iOS App Groups — a secure local communication mechanism on your device. No internet connection is used.

Children's privacy

Spark is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated date. Continued use of Spark after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at support@tastyjams.com or via the Spark support page.
