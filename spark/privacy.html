Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Overview

Spark: ADHD Habit Tracker ("the App") is developed by Tasty Jam ("we", "us"), a sole proprietorship based in British Columbia, Canada. This policy applies to Spark on both iOS and Android.

Data we collect

Your habit data — habits, completions, streaks, and reminders — is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to the cloud so your data is available across all your devices. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

The data we collect or handle includes:

  • Habit data — habit names, completion records, and streak history. On iOS this is synced via Apple iCloud (CloudKit). On Android this is synced via Firebase (Google Cloud). See the Cloud Sync section below.
  • Purchase data — handled entirely by the platform store (Apple App Store on iOS, Google Play on Android). We receive only an anonymised entitlement flag confirming your subscription status; we never receive your payment details.
  • Crash reports — if you opt in to share diagnostics with Apple (iOS) or Google (Android), anonymised crash data may be shared with us via App Store Connect or Google Play Console.
  • Support contact — if you contact us via the support form, we collect the name, email, and message you provide solely to respond to your inquiry.

Cloud sync

iOS: Habit data is synced across your Apple devices using Apple's iCloud CloudKit. This sync is end-to-end encrypted and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Tasty Jam has no access to data stored in your private iCloud container.

Android: Habit data is synced across your Android devices using Firebase (a Google Cloud service). Data is transmitted securely and stored in your private Firebase database, governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Tasty Jam accesses this data only as needed to deliver the sync feature and does not use it for any other purpose.

You can disable cloud sync at any time from the app's Settings. When disabled, data is stored locally on-device only.

What we don't collect

  • No advertising SDKs or third-party analytics trackers
  • No sale of data to third parties
  • No access to contacts, camera, photos, or location
  • No user profiling or advertising data

Notifications

Reminders are scheduled as local notifications on your device. On iOS they are handled by the system notification framework; on Android they use AlarmManager. No habit data is sent to any server to deliver notifications.

Subscriptions

Spark Pro subscriptions are processed by Apple (iOS) or Google Play (Android). We never see or store your payment information. On iOS, management and cancellation are handled through your Apple ID settings. On Android, they are handled through your Google Play account.

Apple Watch & widgets (iOS only)

On iOS, habit data shared with the Apple Watch app and home screen widget is transferred via iCloud and iOS App Groups — secure mechanisms on your device and iCloud account. No additional data is transmitted beyond the standard iCloud sync described above.

Data retention & deletion

On iOS: Habit data persists in your iCloud account across your devices. Deleting the App removes local data, but iCloud data remains until you delete it from your iCloud settings.

On Android: Habit data is stored on-device and synced to your private Firebase database. Uninstalling the App removes local data. To delete your cloud data, use Settings → Delete My Data inside the App, or contact us at support@tastyjam.ca and we will delete it within 30 days.

Children's privacy

Spark does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App does not require an account and does not collect any personally identifiable information beyond what you explicitly enter as habit names.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at support@tastyjam.ca.
