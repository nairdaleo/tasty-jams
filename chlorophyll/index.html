Frequently Asked Questions

What is Chlorophyll Pro?
Chlorophyll Pro is a one-time purchase (no subscription) that unlocks the photo growth timeline, fertilizer stats, CSV & PDF export, Siri shortcuts, and home screen widgets. The free tier is generous and stays free forever — unlimited plants, unlimited care tasks, full reminders, and complete care history.
Does it work without an internet connection?
Yes. Chlorophyll works fully offline. iCloud syncs your data in the background when connected, but the app never requires a network connection to use.
Does Chlorophyll identify plants or suggest care schedules?
No — intentionally. Chlorophyll is a manual tracker. You know your plants best. We give you the tools to log what you do and remind you when to do it again. No AI, no algorithm overriding your judgment.
How do I restore my Pro purchase?
Open Chlorophyll → tap the menu → Restore Purchase. Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID and works across all your devices. Pro also includes Family Sharing.
Is my data backed up?
Yes — via iCloud. Your plants, tasks, care logs, and photos sync automatically. No data is stored on our servers.
Does Chlorophyll work on Mac and iPad?
Yes. Chlorophyll is a universal app — iPhone, iPad, and Mac all use the same purchase. On iPad and Mac, the plant list appears in a sidebar with the detail view on the right.
