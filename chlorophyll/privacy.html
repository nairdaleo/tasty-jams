Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Overview

Chlorophyll is a plant care tracker built with privacy by default, developed by Tasty Jam ("we", "us"), a sole proprietorship based in British Columbia, Canada. This policy applies to Chlorophyll on both iOS and Android. Your plant data — care logs, photos, and reminders — is stored on your device and optionally synced to the cloud. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

What we don't collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number) — no account is required
  • No usage analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No advertising identifiers (no ads, ever)
  • No location data
  • No health or fitness data
  • No crash-reporting SDK

Data we handle

Chlorophyll does not collect any personal information for advertising or analytics purposes. The data we handle includes:

  • Plant data — plant names, species, care tasks, care logs, fertilizer notes, and photos you enter in the App. This data is stored locally and optionally synced to the cloud (see Cloud Sync below).
  • Purchase data — handled entirely by the platform store (Apple App Store on iOS, Google Play on Android). We receive only an anonymised entitlement flag confirming your Pro status; we never receive your payment details.
  • Support contact — if you contact us via the support form, we collect the name, email, and message you provide solely to respond to your inquiry.

Data stored on your device

All plant data you enter is stored locally on your device — using SwiftData on iOS, and Room (SQLite) on Android. This data belongs entirely to you.

Cloud sync

iOS: If you have iCloud enabled, Chlorophyll uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your plant data across your iPhone, iPad, and Mac. This sync is encrypted end-to-end by Apple and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Tasty Jam has no access to data stored in your private CloudKit container.

Android: Chlorophyll uses Firebase (a Google Cloud service) to sync your plant data across your Android devices. To associate your data with your device without requiring an account, Chlorophyll uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication, which creates a persistent anonymous identifier on first launch. This identifier is not linked to your name, email address, or any personal information, and is never shared with Tasty Jam or any third party. Data is transmitted securely and stored in your private Firebase database, governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Tasty Jam accesses this data only as needed to deliver the sync feature and does not use it for any other purpose.

You can disable cloud sync at any time from the app's Settings. When disabled, data is stored locally on-device only.

Photos

When you add plant photos, they are stored as compressed image data in your local on-device store and synced via the cloud infrastructure described above (iCloud on iOS, Firebase on Android). Photos are never uploaded to any server operated by Tasty Jam.

Notifications

Chlorophyll schedules care reminders as local notifications on your device. On iOS they use Apple's notification framework; on Android they use AlarmManager. No plant data is sent to any server to deliver notifications.

Siri & Shortcuts (iOS only)

On iOS, if you use Siri shortcuts with Chlorophyll, Apple's App Intents framework processes your request locally on-device where possible. No plant data is sent to Tasty Jam's servers as a result of using Siri.

In-app purchases

Chlorophyll Pro is a one-time in-app purchase processed entirely by Apple (iOS) or Google Play (Android). Tasty Jam receives only the anonymised aggregate sales information the platform provides to all developers. We do not receive your payment details or any personally identifiable information from the purchase.

Third-party services

The third-party services Chlorophyll uses are:

  • Apple iCloud / CloudKit (iOS) — cloud sync, governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase (Android) — cloud sync, governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • StoreKit / Google Play Billing — in-app purchase processing, governed by the respective platform's policies.

No third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services are integrated.

Data retention & deletion

On iOS: Plant data persists in your iCloud account across your devices as long as your iCloud account is active. Deleting the App removes local data, but iCloud data remains until you manage it from your iCloud settings.

On Android: Plant data is stored on-device and synced to your private Firebase database. Uninstalling the App removes local data. To wipe local data immediately, go to Android Settings → Apps → Chlorophyll → Storage → Clear Storage. To delete your cloud data, use Settings → Delete My Data inside the App, or contact us at support@tastyjam.ca and we will delete it within 30 days.

Children

Chlorophyll does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not require an account and collects no personal information from any user of any age beyond the plant names and care notes you choose to enter.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
