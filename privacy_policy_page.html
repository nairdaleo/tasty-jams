Overview

Tasty Jamz ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Fart Soundboard mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our App.

Information Collection and Use

We want to be crystal clear: Fart Soundboard does not collect any personal information or data from users.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Audio recordings or microphone access
  • Photos or camera access

  • Any other personal or sensitive information

How the App Works

Fart Soundboard is a simple sound playback application that operates entirely on your device. All sound files are included within the app and played locally. No internet connection is required for the app to function, and no data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our App does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. There are no third-party SDKs or tracking mechanisms included in the app.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is suitable for all ages and contains no data collection mechanisms whatsoever.

Data Security

Since we do not collect, store, or transmit any user data, there is no data to secure. Your use of the app is completely private and confined to your device.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data for you to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over the app simply by choosing whether or not to use it.

Contact Us
