Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Overview

Klick ("the App") is developed by Tasty Jam ("we", "us"). We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data collection

Klick does not collect or transmit any personal data to third-party servers. Counter data is synced via iCloud CloudKit, Apple's secure cloud infrastructure. This data is encrypted end-to-end and stored only in your iCloud account.

What we don't collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No health or fitness data

Apple Watch

Counter data syncs between your iPhone and Apple Watch through iCloud CloudKit. All sync data is encrypted end-to-end and does not pass through any third-party servers.

In-app purchases

If you purchase Klick Pro, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Third-party services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data retention

Counter data is synced via iCloud and persists across your devices as long as your iCloud account is active. Deleting the App removes it from a device, but data remains in your iCloud account. To permanently delete your data, you must delete it from all devices and manage your iCloud storage settings. iCloud storage is governed by Apple's privacy and data retention policies.

Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since no personal data is collected from any user, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@tastyjam.ca.
