Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Overview

Klick ("the App") is developed by Tasty Jam ("we", "us"). We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used. The same policy applies to Klick on both iOS and Android.

Data collection

Klick does not collect or transmit any personal data to third-party servers. All counter data — names, values, history, and settings — stays on your device.

On iOS, counter data is optionally synced across your Apple devices via iCloud CloudKit, Apple's end-to-end encrypted cloud infrastructure. On Android, counter data is stored locally in an on-device database (Android Room) and a private preferences store (Android DataStore). No cloud sync is performed on Android.

What we don't collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers (no ads, ever)
  • No location data
  • No health or fitness data
  • No crash-reporting SDK in v1

Apple Watch

On iOS, counter data syncs between your iPhone and Apple Watch through iCloud CloudKit. All sync data is encrypted end-to-end and does not pass through any third-party servers.

In-app purchases

If you purchase Klick Pro, the transaction is processed entirely by the platform store — Apple App Store on iOS, Google Play Billing on Android. We do not receive or store your payment information. The only data we receive from the store is an anonymous entitlement flag saying "this user owns Pro"; that flag is cached on-device in your preferences store.

Third-party services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services on either platform. The only third-party code we call into is the platform's own billing API (StoreKit on iOS, Google Play Billing on Android) to handle in-app purchases, and the platform's in-app review API to optionally prompt you for a rating at milestones.

Data retention

On iOS: Counter data is synced via iCloud and persists across your devices as long as your iCloud account is active. Deleting the App removes it from a device, but data remains in your iCloud account. To permanently delete your data, you must delete it from all devices and manage your iCloud storage settings. iCloud storage is governed by Apple's privacy and data retention policies.

On Android: Counter data lives only on the device where you created it. Uninstalling the App removes all data. You can also wipe data at any time from Android's Settings → Apps → Klick → Storage → Clear Storage.

Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since no personal data is collected from any user, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
