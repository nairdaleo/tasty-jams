Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Overview

Klick ("the App") is developed by Tasty Jam ("we", "us"). We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used. The same policy applies to Klick on both iOS and Android.

Data collection

Klick does not collect or transmit any personal data to third-party servers for advertising or analytics purposes. All counter data — names, values, history, and settings — is stored on your device and optionally synced to the cloud so it is available across all your devices.

Cloud sync

iOS: Counter data is synced across your Apple devices via iCloud CloudKit, Apple's end-to-end encrypted cloud infrastructure. This sync is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Tasty Jam has no access to data stored in your private iCloud container.

Android: Counter data is synced across your Android devices using Firebase (a Google Cloud service). Data is transmitted securely and stored in your private Firebase database, governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Tasty Jam accesses this data only as needed to deliver the sync feature and does not use it for any other purpose.

You can disable cloud sync at any time from the app's Settings. When disabled, data is stored locally in an on-device database (Room) and preferences store (DataStore) on Android, or locally in CoreData on iOS.

What we don't collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers (no ads, ever)
  • No location data
  • No health or fitness data
  • No crash-reporting SDK in v1

Apple Watch & widgets (iOS only)

On iOS, counter data syncs between your iPhone and Apple Watch through iCloud CloudKit. All sync data is encrypted end-to-end and does not pass through any third-party servers.

In-app purchases

If you purchase Klick Pro, the transaction is processed entirely by the platform store — Apple App Store on iOS, Google Play Billing on Android. We do not receive or store your payment information. The only data we receive from the store is an anonymous entitlement flag saying "this user owns Pro"; that flag is cached on-device in your preferences store.

Third-party services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics or advertising services. The third-party services we use are:

  • Apple iCloud / CloudKit (iOS) — cloud sync, governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase (Android) — cloud sync, governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • StoreKit / Google Play Billing — in-app purchase processing, governed by the respective platform's policies.
  • In-app review API — optionally prompts you for a rating at milestones; no personal data is collected by Tasty Jam as part of this.

Data retention & deletion

On iOS: Counter data persists in your iCloud account across your devices. Deleting the App removes local data, but iCloud data remains until you manage it from your iCloud settings.

On Android: Counter data is stored on-device and synced to your private Firebase database. Uninstalling the App removes local data. To delete your cloud data, use Settings → Delete My Data inside the App, or contact us at support@tastyjam.ca and we will delete it within 30 days. You can also wipe local data at any time from Android Settings → Apps → Klick → Storage → Clear Storage.

Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App does not require an account and collects no personally identifiable information beyond the counter names you choose to enter.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@tastyjam.ca.
