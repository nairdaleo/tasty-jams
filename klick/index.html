Frequently Asked Questions

What is Klick?
Klick is a simple multi-counter app. Create counters for anything — reps at the gym, drinks of water, inventory counts, game scores. Tap to increment, swipe to reset. Available on iPhone and Apple Watch.
Does Klick work on Apple Watch?
Yes. Klick includes a Watch complication so you can see and update your counters directly from your wrist without pulling out your phone.
Is Klick free?
Klick will offer a free tier with basic counters. Pro features like unlimited counters, custom icons, and Watch complications will be available via a one-time purchase.
When is Klick launching?
Klick is currently in development. Follow us for updates — we'll announce the launch date soon.
How is my data stored?
Counter data syncs automatically via iCloud to all your devices. No account creation needed — just sign in with your Apple ID. Keep iCloud enabled to protect your data.
