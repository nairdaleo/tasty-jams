Delete My Data

Use this page to permanently delete all your Klick counter data from our cloud servers. Your local data on your device is not affected.

This action is irreversible. Once deleted, your cloud data cannot be recovered. Make sure to export a local backup first if you want to keep your counters.

How to find your User ID

  1. Open Klick on your Android device and go to Settings (tap ⋮ in the top-right corner).
  2. Scroll to the Cloud Sync section. Make sure cloud sync is enabled — your User ID is only assigned when sync is turned on.
  3. Tap Copy User ID. A unique identifier will be copied to your clipboard.
  4. Paste it in the field below and request deletion.

You're about to permanently delete all cloud data associated with User ID . This includes all counters and their history.

Data stored locally on your device will not be deleted.

Data that is deleted

  • All counter names and current values
  • All counter history (tap events)
  • Your anonymous account record

Data that is not deleted by this page

  • Counter data stored locally on your device — to remove this, uninstall Klick.
  • In-app purchase records — these are managed by Google Play and are not stored on our servers.
